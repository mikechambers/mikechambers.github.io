360 Flex in Milan

Monday, March 24, 2008

I just posted this on the on AIR tour blog, and wanted to make a quick note here, but the 360 Flex conference is heading to Milan, Italy. I actually attended the first 360 Flex conference, and it is definitely worth it if you are at all interested in Flex development.

I won’t be able to make the event in Milan this year, but the speaker list is pretty amazing. Check out the website for a complete list, as well as session and registration information.

# Star Wars Destiny FAQ This page contains frequently asked rules questions about the Star Wars Destiny Collectible Card game which have not been addressed in the officially released rules. ## Resources - [Star Wars Destiny Product Page](https://www.fantasyflightgames.com/en/products/star-wars-destiny/) - [Official Star Wars Destiny Forum](https://community.fantasyflightgames.com/forum/510-star-wars-destiny/) - [Star Wars Destiny Official Rules Reference and FAQ](https://images-cdn.fantasyflightgames.com/filer_public/0f/94/0f94b62c-2ead-4eeb-8409-e24bb8aa54ae/swd_rules_reference_12.pdf) (v 1.2 January 24, 2017) ## Questions ### Does Qui-Gon Jinn's ability resolve if he has three shields? Yes. Qui-Gon's ability reads: > Before this character gains 1 or more shields, you may remove 1 of his shields to deal 1 damage to a character. In a series of emails from Lukas Litzsinger (the primary game developer) it is confirmed that there is no difference between "giving" and "gaining" shields, and that Qui-Gon's ability resolves even if he already has three shields.

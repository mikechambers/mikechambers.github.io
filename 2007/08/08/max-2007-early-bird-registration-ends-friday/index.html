MAX 2007 Early Bird Registration Ends Friday

Just a quick fyi, but the MAX 2007 early bird registration ends on Friday. If you have been thinking about attending, now is the time to do so, in order to get the best rate.

I havent had a chance to blog to much (i.e. any) about MAX this year (because of the bus tour), but I think this is going to (hands down) the best MAX ever. Ted Patrick has been put in charge of the conference, and I have been really impressed with the change in focus on the conference.
